# Family Medical Record System

A cloud-based Family Medical Record and Emergency Access Management System developed as a mini project using Microsoft Azure services.

---

## Project Overview

The Family Medical Record System provides a centralized platform where users can securely store and manage medical records of their family members. The system is designed to provide quick access to essential medical information during emergencies while keeping all records organized and easily accessible.

The platform focuses on secure record management rather than medical diagnosis or treatment.

---

## Problem Statement

Medical records are often scattered across paper files, hospital folders, emails, and messaging applications. During emergencies, locating these records becomes difficult and time-consuming.

This project aims to provide a single secure platform for storing and retrieving medical information whenever it is needed.

---

## Objectives

- Store medical records securely.
- Maintain medical profiles for family members.
- Organize insurance documents.
- Store medicine information with expiry dates.
- Extract medicine expiry dates using OCR.
- Generate alerts for medicine and insurance expiry.
- Provide emergency access using QR code and PIN verification.
